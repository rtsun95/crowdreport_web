CommuniVoice

Improve your city!

Are you tired of potholes everywhere? Do you want to improve your city? CommuniVoice provides a simple way to report issues and complaints directly to the city. Forget about the calling 411, and move on into the modern age with the fast and innovative CommuniVoice. With CommuniVoice, you can reports any kind of problems around the city effortlessly. Just choose a category, take a picture and you are set. We save the location and time and send the information right away to the city.
